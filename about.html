About Us

About Salem, CEO

As a business Shifu (master), I help entrepreneurs and small business people become true business owners, where they can both own their business and their life.

Who am I and why did I end up becoming a Shifu?

At a very young age, I had the entrepreneurial spirit. I started my first business when I was 4 years old as a subcontractor to my brother’s paper route. I was paid 10 cents & a bag of chips every Friday and I felt like a big shot.

Over the next years, I turned revenue streams and profit development into an art form across a variety of industries. I developed a cross-section of successful business platforms and sales strategies for numerous business leaders. It is always exciting to work with small business owners and their executive teams.

In order to help as many entrepreneurs and small business owners, I founded the Collaborative Business Community in the west end of Ottawa called Collab Space. Executive knowledge should be available to all business owners; not just those that can afford it.

Collab Space engenders the philosophy that we are all stronger together. Collab Members appreciate the exceptional level of education and structured collaboration. Members benefit from the strength in numbers by sharing ideas, resources, and solutions.

All along the way, I've helped over 300 people achieve the dream of true business ownership. I am here to help anyone who is driven to succeed.

“Moving you from surviving to thriving.”

Is your business falling behind the competition? Our primary goal is to help you refocus your energy from unprofitable activities to ones that will help you blow past your competition. Our team of experts will help you create and implement strategies and systems to support your positive growth.

Story:

Most of us start a business based on an idea for either a service or product we felt was missing from the marketplace. Some of us get into business to create a job for ourselves, few of us, plan any further than, You might say or think: "hey I am going to give this a try and see what happens, if I am lucky, I will have work for several years and may be able to create a life and some jobs along the way. I just need to put food on my table."

You never really gave it any more thought than that, you will go along and build your business as a one-person show who needs to do everything from finding the customers, to providing the quotes, to doing the work yourself, to the final billing and accounting and submitting taxes to the government, heck you are even the one who answers the phone while in the office and are returning calls at the end of a long day after doing all the work yourself. You are barely surviving in business. So, if this sounds remotely like you, you are in the right place.

My team and I offer our clients a wide array of services from program evaluations, executive coaching, leadership training to assisting them prepare for an initial public offering.
